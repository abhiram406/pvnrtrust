About PVNR Trust

Our Story

The P.V.N.R Trust was established with the mission to address blood shortages and ensure its availability in even the most remote regions of Telangana. This visionary initiative, led by the PV family, is rooted in their grandfather's dream and vision. Backed by decades of medical expertise, the family has taken significant steps to make this goal a reality.

In 2018, the Trust inaugurated a Blood Bank in Gajwel Mandal, Siddipet District, Telangana, inspired by the vision of former Prime Minister and Bharat Ratna Awardee Shri. P.V. Narasimha Rao. Operating 24/7, the Blood Bank serves the rural population tirelessly. In September 2022, the Trust expanded its reach by launching a new branch in Hyderabad.

The Blood Bank provides free blood to voluntary donors and patients with conditions like Thalassemia, Sickle Cell Disease, Anaemia, and Haemophilia. Additionally, 30% of the blood collected at camps is supplied free to government hospitals, while nominal testing charges, as per government regulations, are applied to other patients.

The Trust addresses not only the procurement of quality blood but also the challenges of hygienic storage and timely distribution. With plans to establish additional blood banks, the P.V.N.R Trust is steadfast in its commitment to ensuring that blood, the 'lifeline' of public health, is always accessible and of the highest quality.

Born on June 28, 1921, into a Telugu Brahmin family in Andhra Pradesh, P.V. Narasimha Rao was a gifted polyglot fluent in 17 languages. The Grandfather's Dream

A conversation that shaped a life's work.

The singular goal of "service to all" was the driving force of Shri P.V. Narasimha Rao's life, a vision he instilled in his family.

"

I clearly remember the day I told my grandfather I had become a doctor — he was ecstatic. But the first thing he said wasn't 'congratulations' or 'I'm proud of you.' Instead, he said, 'Service is what you should do — service without filters or discrimination, especially in rural areas. This is my dream, what I've dedicated my life to. Will you carry it forward?'

— Dr. N.V. Sudha Kiran, CEO

Dr. Kiran reflects, "Establishing this Trust and the Blood Centres, and engaging in the work we do every day, brings me immense fulfillment. Each morning, I wake up with a sense of purpose and a commitment to make a difference. And each night, I go to bed with a smile, knowing I've done my part. I owe it all to my grandfather."

Legacy

A transformative leader.

As Prime Minister, P.V. Narasimha Rao spearheaded groundbreaking economic reforms that liberalized and opened up the Indian economy, propelling the nation into an era of rapid growth and establishing its position as a global economic power. He also implemented significant policy changes in education, infrastructure, and governance, while working to strengthen India's relations with neighboring countries such as China and Pakistan.

Renowned for his intellectual acumen, pragmatic leadership, and unwavering commitment to national development, Narasimha Rao remained a respected and influential figure in Indian politics. After retiring, he returned to his passion for writing and scholarship, authoring several books on Indian philosophy, culture, and history. He passed away on December 23, 2004, leaving behind a legacy as one of India's most visionary and transformative leaders.

Before you donate

The Complete Guide

Simple facts, clear answers, and practical tips — so you can walk in informed and walk out proud.

Who Can Donate?

  • Any healthy adult can donate blood
  • Men every 3 months, women every 6 months
  • Weight above 45 kgs
  • Age between 18 and 65 years
  • No serious diseases or active medication issues
  • Not currently pregnant or breastfeeding

Benefits

  • Free health checkup — pulse, BP, weight
  • Reveals potential diseases through screening
  • Early detection helps you get better sooner
  • Boosts both mental and physical health
  • Helps create fresh blood in your body
  • The joy of saving up to 3 lives

Before Donating

  • Stay hydrated throughout the day
  • Have a balanced meal beforehand
  • Have a good night's sleep — don't overwork
  • Lay off drinks and smoking
  • Increase consumption of iron-rich foods
  • Avoid fatty foods

While & After

  • Inform staff if currently on medication
  • Relax — bring a book or listen to music
  • Rest 5–7 minutes after the process
  • Consume the refreshments we provide
  • Avoid strenuous activities for 24 hours
  • No drinks or smoking for 24 hours

A Transformative Initiative

Mission One Crore
Blood Donors

Our Goal
0
Voluntary donors ready to respond to urgent blood requirements nearby.
A growing movement Nationwide

Blood is typically collected through two primary methods: blood donation camps organized by blood centers and voluntary donors who visit blood centers to donate. Mission One Crore Blood Donors introduces a transformative third approach.

Conceived by our CEO, Dr. N.V. Sudha Kiran, the mission aspires to build a nationwide network of one crore voluntary donors ready to respond to urgent blood requirements nearby. We collect basic donor information — location, blood group, last donation date, and consent to be contacted in emergencies.

When a specific blood group is unavailable nearby, our team uses the database to identify donors in the vicinity. Once a donor agrees, our team coordinates collection and delivery — ensuring quality and timeliness are never compromised.
